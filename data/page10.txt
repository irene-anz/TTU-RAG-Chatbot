Tatung University International Students Scholarship
The purpose of the scholarship is to promote internationalization and to encourage outstanding international undergraduate/graduate students to pursue their studies at Tatung University.
Applicants shall not receive scholarships from any other R.O.C. government agencies.

Self-Paid Items
Obligations
Awards are suspended during Leave of Absence or Dropout. Extended study students are ineligible.
No double funding with Government scholarships.
Insurance, Winter/Summer dormitory stay, and PC/Network fees are not covered by the scholarship programs.
Awardees must assist in International Affairs. Fraudulent information leads to revocation and full repayment.
