Tatung University (TTU) is a teaching and research-oriented private university highly recognized for its engineering and management programs. Having developed side by side with Tatung Company, the leading electric and electronic multinational company with annual sales of 10 billion US dollars and 40,000 employees worldwide, TTU has placed a special emphasis on practical and professional learning. Over the years, it has a proven track of success in educating and training talented engineers, business professionals, and industrial leaders.
