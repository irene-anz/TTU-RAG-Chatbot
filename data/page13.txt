International Dormitory
On-campus dormitory is guaranteed for International students.
If you would like to apply for accommodation, select “I want to apply for a room in a student residence” for the Accommodation option in the Online Application System while applying for admission.
The International Dormitory for one semester costs NT$ 20,500 (Not including summer and winter break payments)
Environment:
Personal Facilities:
Each student is assigned to a four-person room, equipped with one bed, desk, desk lamp, chair, and wardrobe per person. Each room also includes air conditioning (pay-per-use).
Public Facilities:
The public area is equipped with a refrigerator, water dispenser, microwave, rice cooker, and oven for students' use.
Paid Facilities (Coin-operated):
Washing machines, spin dryers, tumble dryers, and vending machines are available.
Meals, Mattresses, Bedding, and Towels are not included in the TTU student dorm. You can purchase the needed items in a store nearby TTU, including new bedding after your arrival.
