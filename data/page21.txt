INTRODUCTION TO
TATUNG UNIVERSITY (TTU)
Tatung University (TTU) is a teaching and research-oriented
private university highly recognized for its engineering and
management programs. Having developed side by side with
Tatung Company, the leading electric and electronic
multinational company with annual sales of 10 billion US
dollars and 40,000 employees worldwide, TTU has placed a
special emphasis on practical and professional learning. Over
the years, it has a proven track of success in educating and
training talented engineers, business professionals, and
industrial leaders.
LOCATION HISTORY
Tatung University is located in the heart of Taipei, adjacent to Tatung University traces its origins to the vision of Mr. Shan-
Taipei Expo Park and Taipei Fine Arts Museum. The Chih Lin, founder of Hsieh-Chih Trading Company (the
surrounding area features a vibrant mix of traditional markets predecessor of Tatung Company). Believing that education is
and diverse commercial districts, offering both excellent essential to sustainable industrial and societal development,
living convenience and a rich cultural and artistic atmosphere. Mr. Lin donated 80% of his personal assets in 1942 to support
The campus enjoys the advantages of urban vitality alongside the establishment of what is now Tatung Senior High School.
a tranquil environment. This commitment laid the foundation for Tatung Junior
College of Technology in 1956, later reorganized as Tatung
Institute of Technology in 1963 and renamed Tatung
University in 1999, continuing a tradition of integrating
education, industry, and innovation.
MISSION
Tatung University has long been recognized for cultivating
scientists, engineers, and business professionals essential to
national development. In recent years, the University has
further strengthened education in the humanities, arts, and
character development, while fostering a warm and supportive
campus environment. It actively promotes bilateral exchange
with international partner institutions and maintains close
industry–academia collaboration with leading enterprises.
Through these efforts, the University nurtures globally
competitive talents with distinctive strengths, fulfilling its
educational mission of “integration of theory and practice,
advancement of research, integrity and diligence, and
contributing to the nation through industry.”

To accommodate international students and students out- of

LIBRARY
The library houses more than 187,930 volumes of books and
215 periodicals. Internet, 60 online databases, and 138777
electronic journals are made easily accessible to all TTU
members.
COMPUTER CENTER
TTU computer center is equipped with the most updated
facilities, such as Gigabit Ethernet Switch (Cisco 6509), a
wireless switch (Cisco and Aruba), an IP PBX system (Alcatel
Omni Switch), and more than forty rack servers. Combined
with two PC classrooms and two distance learning
classrooms, the wired and wireless networks at TTU also
enable its students and faculties to conduct substantial
scientific and scholastic researches.
TTU computer center had passed ISO9000, BS7799 and
ISO27001 certifications to reach the worldwide criteria for
information security and management. We continuously strive
to provide efficient IT support and safe IT environment for
teaching, research and administration.
